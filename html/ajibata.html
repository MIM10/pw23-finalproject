Ajibata

Ajibata is a sub-district located in Toba Samosir Regency, North Sumatra, Indonesia. Ajibata Subdistrict is the result of expansion from Lumban Julu Subdistrict based on Toba Samosir Regency Regional Regulation No. 7 of 2002. Ajibata Sub-district is also included in the Lake Toba region, and has access to Ajibata Port which is one of the ports on Lake Toba. Ajibata Port has the port code Lake Toba and is one of the main entrances to visit Lake Toba. So, Ajibata is a sub-district in Toba Samosir Regency, North Sumatra, located in the Lake Toba region and has Ajibata Port as the main entrance to Lake Toba.
