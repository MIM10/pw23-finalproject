Lampung

Lampung is a province located at the southern tip of Sumatra Island, Indonesia. The province has cultural diversity, natural beauty, and historical heritage that is worth visiting. Here is a brief description of Lampung: Lampung is famous for its natural beauty, especially Lake Ranau which is the second largest lake in Sumatra. The lake is surrounded by green mountains and stunning scenery. In addition, Lampung also has beautiful beaches such as Tanjung Setia Beach and Kiluan Beach which are famous for the presence of dolphins.

Apart from its natural beauty, Lampung also has a rich culture. Lampung traditional clothing has its own uniqueness and beauty. Lampung men's traditional clothing consists of a shirt with sleeves, sarong, headband, and sandals. Meanwhile, women's traditional Lampung clothing consists of brackets, sarongs, shawls, and headdresses.

Lampung traditional houses also have their own uniqueness. Lampung traditional houses are classified as stilt houses with the body of the house not directly attached to the ground, but supported by poles. Lampung traditional houses have designs and ornaments that contain spiritual meaning and life values.
