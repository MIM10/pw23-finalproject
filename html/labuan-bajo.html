Labuan Bajo

Labuan Bajo, a hidden paradise in eastern Indonesia, invites visitors to experience natural wonders and breathtaking adventures. Located in Komodo District, West Manggarai Regency, East Nusa Tenggara Province, Labuan Bajo enchants with its extraordinary natural beauty. As one of Indonesia's five Super Priority Destinations, Labuan Bajo offers a gateway to the famous Komodo National Park, where the ancient Komodo dragons roam the islands of Rinca and Komodo.

The destination not only amazes with its land biodiversity, but also with its underwater beauty. Islands like Padar, Kelor and Kanawa offer white sandy beaches, crystal clear waters and an underwater environment rich in marine life. Snorkeling and diving activities here provide an unforgettable experience to witness the beauty of coral reefs and colorful fish in the mesmerizing tropical waters.

In addition, Labuan Bajo also offers adventure experiences such as trekking to Cunca Wulang Waterfall and live-on-board, where visitors can stay on a pinisi boat for a few days while exploring the beautiful surrounding islands. Rangko Cave with its turquoise saltwater pools and sunset sky views from Bukit Cinta, Amelia Peak and Silvia Peak add to its appeal as a tourist destination that not only captivates the eye, but also pampers the soul. Labuan Bajo, a place where adventure and natural beauty blend harmoniously.
